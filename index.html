How We Build

Our Technology
& Process

A vertically integrated approach — from materials research to chip architecture — built for India's climate, talent, and scale.

01

Materials & Substrate Research

Pioneering work on Silicon Carbide and Gallium Nitride substrates suited for high-performance, high-temperature environments critical to defence and industrial applications.

02

Next-Gen Chip Architecture

Custom logic & mixed-signal architectures designed for AI inference, edge computing, and autonomous systems — optimised from the ground up, not ported from legacy designs.

03

Fabrication Innovation

Building a domestic fabrication facility with modular clean-room architecture, enabling rapid process iteration at scales inaccessible to multinational foundries in India today.

04

Advanced Packaging

3D chiplet integration and heterogeneous packaging technologies that close the performance gap with global leaders while enabling unique form factors for emerging use cases.

05

Deep Tech Infrastructure

Proprietary EDA toolchains, characterisation labs, and testing infrastructure designed to support the full chip lifecycle — from first silicon to production-grade reliability.

06

Talent & IP Engine

A co-located research institute developing India's next generation of chip engineers — combined with an aggressive IP strategy to build defensible technological advantages.

Where We're Going

The HAVOC
Roadmap

A phased approach from research excellence to full-scale domestic production — each milestone building irreversible momentum.

2025

Phase I

R&D Foundation & Team Assembly

Establishing the core research team, securing lab space, and initiating substrate research programs. Forming strategic partnerships with IITs and international research institutions.

In Progress
2026

Phase II

First Silicon & IP Portfolio

Taping out first proprietary chip designs through partner foundries. Filing key patents. Completing proof-of-concept for novel chip architecture and establishing the IP portfolio baseline.

Upcoming
2027

Phase III

Pilot Fabrication Facility

Constructing India's first privately-owned advanced semiconductor pilot fab. Initial process node targeting 28nm with a roadmap to sub-10nm. First commercial partnerships signed.

Planned
2029

Phase IV

Full-Scale Production & Export

Scaling to full production capacity. Supplying defence, automotive, and AI infrastructure sectors domestically. Beginning export operations to Southeast Asia and Middle Eastern markets.

Planned
