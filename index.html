The Stakes

Why semiconductors
define nations

Semiconductors are not merely components — they are the foundation of national security, economic sovereignty, and technological leadership. Every AI model, every defence system, every digital economy depends on chips.

Yet India imports nearly 100% of its semiconductor needs — there is no domestic advanced chip manufacturing today. A disruption in global supply chains doesn't just slow growth — it halts it. HAVOC exists to change that equation permanently.

How We Build

Our Technology
& Process Stack

A vertically integrated approach — from materials research to chip architecture — built for India's climate, talent, and scale.

01 — MATERIALS

SiC & GaN Substrate Research

Pioneering work on Silicon Carbide and Gallium Nitride substrates for high-performance environments critical to defence and industrial applications.

02 — ARCHITECTURE

Next-Gen Chip Design

Custom logic & mixed-signal architectures for AI inference, edge computing, and autonomous systems — optimised from the ground up.

03 — FABRICATION

Modular Clean-Room Fab

Building a domestic fabrication facility with modular clean-room architecture, enabling rapid process iteration at scales inaccessible to multinationals in India.

04 — PACKAGING

3D Chiplet Integration

Heterogeneous packaging technologies that close the performance gap with global leaders while enabling unique form factors for emerging use cases.

05 — INFRASTRUCTURE

Proprietary EDA Toolchain

Custom characterisation labs and testing infrastructure designed to support the full chip lifecycle — from first silicon to production reliability.

06 — TALENT

IP & Research Engine

A co-located research institute developing India's next generation of chip engineers, combined with an aggressive IP strategy for defensible advantages.

Where We're Going

The HAVOC
Roadmap

Four phases from research foundation to full-scale domestic production and export.

2026
Phase I
In Progress

R&D Foundation & Team Assembly

Establishing the core research team, securing lab space, and initiating substrate research programs. Forming strategic partnerships with IITs and international research institutions.

30% complete
2027
Phase II
Upcoming

First Silicon & IP Portfolio

Taping out first proprietary chip designs through partner foundries. Filing key patents. Completing proof-of-concept for novel chip architecture and establishing the IP baseline.

2028
Phase III
Planned

Pilot Fabrication Facility

Constructing India's first privately-owned advanced semiconductor pilot fab. Initial process node targeting 28nm with a roadmap to sub-10nm. First commercial partnerships signed.

2030
Phase IV
Planned

Full-Scale Production & Export

Scaling to full production capacity. Supplying defence, automotive, and AI infrastructure sectors domestically. Beginning export operations to Southeast Asia and the Middle East.
